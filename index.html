About Me

I am Tse Ho Tung, a travel enthusiast. I love traveling around the world because I love exploring different country’s cultural and I would like to walk out of my comfort zone. I think that each time of my journey would be my valuable memory in my life, and it could help me to broaden my horizon. I think that living in an unfamiliar country, I could know more about the country, not only the information that I read from the internet but also have firsthand experience. These are the reasons why I love traveling.

United Kingdom

United Kingdom, London | 14/07/2019-03/08/2019

I went to London because I joined a study tour and I had a 3-week summer course. The school had taken students to sightseeing too. It was a meaningful summer.

I went to the British Museum which is a public institution dedicated to human history, art and culture. It is a must to go if you come London.

I went to Warner Bros. Studio Tour London – The Making of Harry Potter. It is a filmmaking exhibit. It returns the classic scene of the Harry Potter. If you are a Harry Potter lover, you should not miss it! I was crazy at that day!

This is fish and chips. It is a must again if you come to UK. It is a popular dish in UK. It is very tasty, and the size is really big! You will have to share with someone.

Singapore

Singapore | 07/06/2019-11/06/2019

I went to Singapore with my friends. We were all the 1st time going to Singapore. I like traveling with them!

We went to the Gardens by the Bay which is a nature park with the aim of raising the quality of life by enhancing greenery and flora in the city. I think that the design is new and special.

We went to Universal Studio Singapore. The size of the park is not big and the rides inside are not really exciting but we still had a fun day.

This is Bak kut the which is a pork rib dish cooked in broth popularly served in Singapore. I like it very much but the taste of it is not generally accepted. But if you didn’t try it before, please go ahead. You might be surprised!

Korea

Korea, Seoul | 01/01/2019-07/01/2019

I went to Seoul with my friend. It was the 2nd time I went to Seoul. I like Korea’s weather because it won't feel wet when comparing to Hong Kong.

We went to Gyeongbokgung which was the main royal palace of the Joseon dynasty. We had borrowed hanbok for taking photo. It is common to see people wearing hanbok to the Gyeongbokgung because if people wearing hanbok, they don’t need to paid for the entrance fee.

We went to skiing on the 3rd day of the trip. It costs only HKD600 for the skiing day tour. There will be a coach teaching you. You don’t have to worry too much. It was the first time I go skiing!

This is Gimbap, a Korean dish made from cooked rice and other ingredients that are rolled in gim—dried sheets of laver seaweed—and served in bite-sized slices. Gimbap is very popular that you can easily find in Korea. I like it very much. I eat almost everyday.
